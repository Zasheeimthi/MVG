MVG | Stretch

SKU: FAIKHEGXJO

Why Choose Us?

This efficient digital wayfinding terminal, also known as a KIOSK, features a large multitouch screen of 43.5″ and provides a straightforward and effective solution for guiding your customers through your space. It also offers our tailor-made “Terminal UX” experience that has been developed over many years to optimize people’s navigation with ease. All necessary components are already built-in, such as internet (LTE) and a media player, making it effortless and quick to set up. Simply plug it in, and it’s ready to use.

Moreover, the digital wayfinding terminal can be combined with our MVG | NavApp, which provides a state-of-the-art augmented reality navigation experience, taking wayfinding to the next level. The transfer process is wireless, seamless, and fast, eliminating the need for customers to install anything since it runs directly in the browser, making it a hassle-free option for enhancing their mobile wayfinding experience.

With the purchase of this product, you’ll have access to our digital wayfinding and digital signage content system managers. This allows you to not only easily manage the wayfinding system but also customize the screensaver and share important promotions and announcements with your audience.

4. Poster App License

The digital poster's UX/UI is designed to emphasize multiple features, categories, and relevant information on a building map for a quick overview. It displays a list of tenants and a numbered map highlighting all the Point of Interests (POIs). Additionally, it comes with an integrated digital signage area or "banner" where users can manage ads and other information. The entire application can be accessed wirelessly by scanning its QR code to acquire the MVG | NavApp. The content can also be automated entirely by synchronizing the information with the user's current CMS system. The optimal performance of the application is observed on larger 4K displays, but it is still suitable for use in 1080 resolutions in either portrait or landscape mode.
