Digital Signage

Communicate effectively with customers both indoors and outdoors, regardless of the environment. Ensures that your brand messaging and product offerings are consistently visible and accessible.

With our solution, you can transform the way your customers interact with your brand and enhance their overall experience.

Tailor-made solution

Our signage software is designed to be easy to set up, use, and manage. With our solution, you have full control over your digital fleet of screens of any type. You can choose from different levels of control, ranging from designers’ view only to administration, client management, and content management systems for marketing/designers.

Our unique technology enables you to connect what people are searching for with relevant ads that show automatically on the screen(s). This technology is a game-changer as it can determine what people are searching for and their location, allowing it to display the most appropriate content. This feature can increase engagement and drive revenue, making it an ideal solution for businesses of all sizes.

Unique selling point

For all our digital signage customers, we advice to integrate digital wayfinding into the solution. The reason behind this is the valuable insights it provides to our developed digital signage manager. By connecting to digital wayfinding, you gain real-time data on what people are actively searching for. This data can be seamlessly combined with our “sync” plugins to align search behavior with relevant ads and information that precisely matches users’ needs.

No more random ad placements or relying on static numbers. Now, you can harness the extensive programmatic knowledge of digital wayfinding to create a truly unique and revenue-generating approach. Simultaneously, you provide your audience with the accurate information they seek.
